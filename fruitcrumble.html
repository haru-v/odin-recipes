Fruit crumble recipe

My mom really hated this recipe but i thought it was good, the contrarian in me wanted to make a website out of it

Ingredients

This is all you need to make this sick ass crumble beast

  • Flour (i really don't know how much just follow ur intuition)
  • Oats (same amount you used for flour)
  • 100 g of butter ? at least ?
  • 250g of the fruit of your choice (don't use bananas you sick ass fuck)
  • two soup spoons of sugar (white, brown , yellow, use any ethnicity you want)
  • two coffespoons of cinnamon
  • a pinch of salt
  • 2 egg yolks

The process

  • Get you a bowl, add in your flower and your oats, your salt and 1 spoon of your sugar and finally your coffeespoon of cinnamon and mix until combined
  • add your two eggyolks and mix
  • Make sure your butter is really cold,get you a cheese grater and grate your butter on the mixture
  • it's optional but you can add some drops of apple cider vinegar
  • Mix between your hands until you get a couscous like texture and for fuck's sake don't knead the damn thing
  • Now place your fruits unto a pan and add in the other spoon of sugar and of cinnamon
  • Mix your fruits on low heat for no more than 6 minutes, turn off the heat get it a lid to trap the moist
  • Place in some parchement paper unto your baking tray and add half of your initial mix and press it down evenly
  • As a second layer add in your cooked fruits
  • Lastly finish it off with the rest of your floats mix (cause flour+oats duh) and press down gently
  • Place it in the oven for 20 minutes

Bitch we cooked now we serve

you can use any toppings you'd like but here are some recommendations:

  • Crushed nuts fruity crumble
  • Whipped cream fruity crumble
  • fresh fruits
  • some honey or any other kind of syrup
  • Dried fruits
